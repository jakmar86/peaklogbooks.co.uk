About Peak Log Books

Journals and companions
for outdoor enthusiasts

Peak Log Books publishes dedicated log books for Scotland's great hill lists. We believe that the record of a climb, the weather, the companions, the route taken, is as important as the climb itself.

Every book in the Peak Log Books series is designed to last a lifetime. Pre-filled with the details that matter, our log books give hillwalkers the structure to record their journeys properly, without the effort of researching each summit from scratch.

Peak Log Books is a trading name of Dellally Limited, registered in England and Wales.
